## FAQ

**Q: Is this really free?**
A: Yes. SpendLens is completely free to use. No credit card, no login, no catch. We make money when users with large savings opportunities buy discounted AI credits through Credex.

**Q: How accurate is the audit?**
A: The audit engine uses official pricing from each vendor's pricing page, verified weekly. All savings calculations are shown with full reasoning so you can verify them yourself.

**Q: Do you store my data?**
A: We only store your data if you choose to save your report by entering your email. The audit itself runs entirely in your browser — nothing is sent to our servers until you opt in.

**Q: What AI tools do you support?**
A: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, OpenAI API, Anthropic API, Gemini, and Windsurf. We add new tools every week based on user requests.

**Q: What is Credex?**
A: Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise, and others — sourced from companies that overforecast or pivoted. The discount is real and substantial. For users with significant savings opportunities, SpendLens will surface Credex as a way to capture even more savings.
