# Unit Economics

## What Is a Converted Lead Worth to Credex?

Credex sells discounted AI credits. Assume:
- Average deal size: $5,000 in credits purchased
- Credex margin: ~15% = $750 gross profit per customer
- Average customer buys 3x per year = $2,250 LTV per customer

Conservative estimate: **$750 - $2,250 per converted customer**

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) by Channel

| Channel | Est. Users/Month | Conversion to Lead | CAC |
|---------|-----------------|-------------------|-----|
| Hacker News Show HN | 2,000 visits | 2% = 40 leads | $0 |
| Reddit posts | 500 visits | 3% = 15 leads | $0 |
| X/Twitter organic | 1,000 visits | 1.5% = 15 leads | $0 |
| Credex existing customers | 200 contacts | 20% = 40 leads | $0 |
| Word of mouth / shareable URL | 300 visits | 4% = 12 leads | $0 |

**Total: ~120 leads/month at $0 CAC (organic only)**
